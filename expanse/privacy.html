Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Neu Expense – Budget Tracker ("the App") is developed by OZ Lab. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App is designed to work primarily offline. Your financial records, including expenses, income, categories, budgets, and settings, are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or back up your data.

We do not require account registration and do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, address, or phone number.

How Your Information Is Used

  • To store and organize your financial records.
  • To calculate budgets, statistics, and spending insights.
  • To restore your data from backups that you create.
  • To provide app functionality.

Data Storage

Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly export or back it up using features provided by the App.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Google Play Services

If you purchase premium features, payments are securely processed by Google Play. We do not receive your payment card details.

Backups and Exports

If you export or back up your data, you are responsible for protecting those files. They may contain your financial information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the App and its data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may delete your financial data at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App. Exported backup files remain under your control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
