Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Neu Gallery, developed by One2 Labs.

Information Collection and Use

Neu Gallery is built with a privacy-first philosophy.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your photos, videos, or personal information with us or any third parties.

To provide gallery features, the app may request access to:

  • Photos and videos stored on your device
  • Files and media (where required by Android)
  • Notifications (optional)

These permissions are used only to provide the requested functionality. Your media remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

Log Data

Neu Gallery does not collect any log data.

  • No analytics
  • No crash reporting
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage tracking
  • No telemetry

Cookies & Tracking

Neu Gallery does not use cookies, advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Security

Since Neu Gallery does not collect or transmit your personal information, your photos, videos, and albums remain under your control on your device.

We are committed to keeping the app private, secure, and free from unnecessary data collection.

Open Source

Neu Gallery is built using open-source software and embraces the values of transparency, privacy, and community-driven development.

We appreciate the open-source community for advancing software that respects user privacy, freedom, and transparency.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Last updated date.
