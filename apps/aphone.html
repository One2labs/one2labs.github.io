Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to A Phone, developed by One2 Labs.

Information Collection and Use

A Phone is designed with a privacy-first philosophy.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal information with us or any third parties.

To provide core calling and contact management features, the app may request access to:

  • Contacts
  • Phone
  • Call Logs
  • Microphone (when required for calling features)

These permissions are used only on your device. Your information never leaves your device.

Some features allow copying phone numbers to your clipboard. Clipboard contents are managed by your operating system and may be accessible to other apps according to your device's security settings.

Log Data

A Phone does not collect any log data.

  • No analytics
  • No crash reporting
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage tracking
  • No telemetry

Cookies & Tracking

A Phone does not use cookies, advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Security

Your trust is important to us.

Since A Phone does not collect or transmit your personal information, your data remains under your control on your device.

We are committed to keeping the app private, secure, and free from unnecessary data collection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Last updated date.
