Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Neu Messages, developed by One2 Labs.

Information Collection and Use

Neu Messages is built with a privacy-first philosophy. Your messages, contacts, and personal information are processed locally on your device whenever possible.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your SMS, MMS, contacts, phone numbers, or other personal information with us or any third parties.

To provide messaging features, the app may request access to:

  • SMS and MMS messages
  • Contacts
  • Phone state (for multi-SIM support)
  • Notifications
  • Internet (only for MMS and Google Play Billing)

These permissions are used only to provide the requested functionality. Your personal data remains on your device.

Features such as OTP detection, phone number extraction, and contact suggestions are processed entirely on your device. No message content or extracted information is uploaded or shared.

Some features allow copying text, verification codes, or phone numbers to your clipboard. Clipboard contents are managed by your operating system and may be accessible to other applications according to your device's security settings.

Log Data

Neu Messages does not collect any log data.

  • No analytics
  • No crash reporting
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage tracking
  • No telemetry

Internet Usage

Internet access is used only when required for:

  • Sending and receiving MMS through your mobile carrier.
  • Processing purchases through Google Play Billing.

These services do not transmit your SMS messages, contacts, or personal information to One2 Labs.

Cookies & Tracking

Neu Messages does not use cookies, advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Security

Since Neu Messages processes your data locally and does not collect or transmit your personal information, your data remains under your control on your device.

We are committed to keeping the app private, secure, and free from unnecessary data collection.

Open Source

Neu Messages is built using open-source software and embraces the values of transparency, privacy, and community-driven development.

We appreciate the open-source community for advancing software that respects user privacy, freedom, and transparency.
